AI for the Future:
Implementation and Networking of AI Smart Hospital

Project Description

The new Kai Tak New Acute Hospital is opening. Imagine as a world-class "Smart Hospital", AI will be crucial for improving patient outcomes. Your team are asked as IT consultants with designing an AI Patient Monitoring System.

This system will connect various IOT smart sensors (monitoring heart rate, blood pressure, etc.) from every patient to a central AI. The AI will analyze data in real-time to alert nurses before emergencies happen. Your task is to create a presentation proposing a plan for this system, focusing on the network, data, and security required to make it a reality.

Guidelines for Submission

  • Grouping: A group of 1 to 5 students.
  • Duration: Maximum 10 minutes
  • Format: Recorded presentation video in MP4 format or YouTube Links + 1 citation document

Submission Deadline: 17 November 2025, 23:59

  • Type your complete team lists and full citation with an attached docx / google document.
